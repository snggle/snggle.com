FAQ

  • What is SNGGLE APP?
    SNGGLE is a first of its kind hardware security & communication system. SNGGLE is an entirely off-line mobile application whose purpose is to keep all your inmost secrets safe within. As the user, you are empowered to make SNGGLE truly yours, to help it protect what's most dear and valuable to you. SNGGLE will always be at your side whether it's a password, a document, a photo, a 2FA code, a private key carrying unfathomable wealth within or the utmost private conversation. No matter what the task at hand, SNGGLE will find a way to make you worry less about your security. SNGGLE is your best friend and a beloved partner in crime that will never judge or betray you, no matter what difficulties and challenges life might have in store for you. The SNGGLE app is an ever-evolving labor of love for true privacy with the utmost consideration for what truly matters to those in need of the most fundamental freedoms for every human being on the planet. With SNGGLE you can truly become your authentic self, express and manifest your hopes, opinions, and desires, all without anyone looking over your shoulder. Whether you are lost or uncertain, SNGGLE will hold your hand, teach and guide you toward the best possible practices to keep you, your friends and your family safe. Thanks to its shapeshifting nature the app can accompany you in your daily routine, at work and at leisure. SNGGLE will change its form to give you maximum power in executing the toughest security tasks without making its true capacity apparent to the outside world.
  • What exact functionalities does SNGGLE have?
    SNGGLE's function is an ever-lasting and evolving conversation on security and privacy. Its initial set of features will consist of:
    Secrets Manager - encrypted storage for files, documents, photos, secrets, contacts, 2FA codes, and more, with the ability to fine-tune categories, double- blind passwords, and customize access levels to all your secrets. SNGGLE Secrets Manager is the most sophisticated secrets privacy tool in the industry for managing data that never requires exposure of your device to an internet connection.
    Private Key Vaults - enables the organization of asymmetric keys, signing of transactions and messages, persisting history of every signature generated by the user and derivation of multiple public addresses from seed phrases. SNGGLE Vaults can act as independent hardware wallets protected by individual mnemonic phrases and independent user defined access layers.
    SNGGLE Apps - suite of useful tools essential to maintain your privacy and freedom under any circumstances. Whether you need to securely generate new mnemonics, passwords or create an end-to-end encrypted communication chat, SNGGLE will teach you how and help you accomplish even the most challenging security tasks.
  • How can SNGGLE communicate with the world?
    SNGGLE uses end-to-end encrypted and air-gapped means of communication with other Snggles to transport signatures, messages, documents, passwords or any other secret information to and from devices that require them. With support for both sequential QR codes and a fully hands off experience over our in-house developed audio transmission protocol SNGGLE makes it safe and convenient to interact with the world while remaining fully off-line. Whether it's a trusted website requesting a password or a pending transaction requiring a signature, SNGGLE can detect, present its details to the user for approval and propagate any secret, document or message to any desired and verified destination.
  • How is SNGGLE better than other applications?
    While various privacy applications might offer certain features overlapping with SNGGLE, they often remain online and require installation on all your devices to function, unintentionally divulging all your secrets to anyone who takes control over any single one of your devices in an unauthorized manner. SNGGLE is intended to exist as an extension of your own hand in a single copy with exceptions for optional and encrypted backups. When it comes to dedicated hardware security devices (such as "Ledger", "Trezor" or any others), their sheer physical appearance poses a direct life-threatening risk for the user. SNGGLE never reveals its true form and purpose as it remains hidden in plain sight within your airplane-mode-enabled mobile device, making it risk-free to carry with you to the hinterlands as one of the millions of unassuming apps. Though a user customized access layer, even under the stress of wrench attacks SNGGLE can morph into the optimal form to help you make it out of the worst trouble.
