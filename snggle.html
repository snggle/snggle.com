WHO IS ?

SNGGLE is an energetic creature, dynamic, forever lasting and forever morphing within one's imagination. Its form presents itself to a lucky viewer as brave and resolute, with the single goal to elicit the feeling of power and protection of its beholders. Man's history forever surprises us with unknown secrets and uncertainty about both our past and present reality; wouldn't it be so much easier navigating through its pages if anyone could have their own SNGGLE, patiently awaiting to stretch its helpful hands any time it's needed?
In the cold and darkness of the valley of shadows, SNGGLE brings light and hope to everyone in need. It's your best friend to protect your secrets, your thoughts, your family and friends walking towards their own path to freedom.

Maybe one day we all can meet SNGGLE and snuggle together to enjoy each other’s warm company offline, looking at the stars in a clear night sky, far away from the noise of our over-networked, chaotic world and the dangers of novice technologies.
