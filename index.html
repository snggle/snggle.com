Is a first of it's kind hardware security & communication system. This is an entirely off-line mobile application whose purpose is to keep all your inmost secrets safe.

Whether it's a password, a document, a photo, a 2FA code, a private key carrying billion dollars within, or the utmost private conversation you need to keep safe, SNGGLE will be at your side keeping your secrets secure.
The SNGGLE app is an ever-evolving labor of love for true privacy with the utmost consideration for what truly matters to those in need of the most fundamental freedoms for every human being on the planet.
SNGGLE uses end-to-end encrypted and air-gapped means of communication with other Snuggles to transport signatures, messages, documents, password or any other secret information to and from devices that require them.
With support for both sequential QR codes and fully hands off experience over in-house developed audio transmission protocol SNGGLE makes it safe and convenient to interact with the world while remaining fully off-line.

How is SNGGLE better than other privacy applications?

While various privacy applications might offer certain features overlapping with SNGGLE , they often remain online and require installation on all your devices to function, unintentionally divulging all your secrets to anyone who takes control over any single one of your devices in an unauthorized manner. SNGGLE is intended to exist as an extension of your own hand in a single copy with exceptions for optional and encrypted backups.
