How is SNGGLE better than other privacy applications?

While various privacy applications might offer certain features overlapping with SNGGLE , they often remain online and require installation on all your devices to function, unintentionally divulging all your secrets to anyone who takes control over any single one of your devices in an unauthorized manner. SNGGLE is intended to exist as an extension of your own hand in a single copy with exceptions for optional and encrypted backups.
